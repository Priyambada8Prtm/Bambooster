About Bambooster

Bambooster is a battery made from bamboo leaves spa (Bambusa vulgaris) to replace the mercury in it. We use used batteries to focus on reusing and recycling to be more environmentally friendly.

Learn more about Bambooster    

Why We Choose
Bambusa vulgaris ?

This product functions to develop Eco Friendly battery using activated carbon from bamboo plant leaf waste (Bambusa vulgaris) as an conventional alternative battery. This research aims to explore the potential and effectiveness of bamboo plant waste which we compare with the mercury found in conventional batteries.

The research results concluded that activated carbon from bamboo leaf waste can be an electrode material that is environmentally friendly and also the result of recycling used batteries that have been thrown away, and is produced in various types of batteries for everyday electronic equipment.

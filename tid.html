Thailand Inventors Day 2024

BITEC - Bangkok

Bambooster will participate in Thailand Inventors Day 2024, on 2-6 February 2024 at the Bangkok International Trade and Exhibition Center.

By bringing our creation to the previous competition at the World Young Inventors Exhibiton 2023 (WYIE), namely a battery made from yellow bamboo (Bambusa Vulgaris) and development in terms of sales and branding which makes this battery not just a prototype battery, but a battery that is ready to be sold to consumers and the existence of a website that allows it to be accessed by people in all parts of the world.

Hi, There !

We created Bambooster as an environmentally friendly battery by using bamboo leaves as its constituent and using used batteries as the battery medium. We are 6 students of SMAN 1 Denpasar, who are aware of the use of environmentally friendly energy for a better future.

Abel, Ira, Nanta, Pram, Vania, and Dekgus have the same vision and mission to continue to innovate in the development of environmentally friendly technology. We hope that the products we make will slowly start to compete with conventional batteries, so that many people will also help protect our environment by using environmentally friendly goods, such as Bambooster.

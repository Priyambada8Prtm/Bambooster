Eco Friendly
Bamboo Battery

This product functions to develop electricity by utilizing active carbon from bamboo plant leaf waste (Bambusa Vulgaris) as an environmentally friendly alternative battery.

This research aims to explore the potential and effectiveness of bamboo plant waste as a supercapacitor material through experimental methods with four different treatments.

The research results concluded that activated carbon from bamboo leaf waste can be an electrode material that is cheap, environmentally friendly and can be used sustainably.

This environmentally friendly alternative battery is also recycled and is produced in various types of batteries for everyday electronic equipment by providing a sustainable resource with minimal environmental impact.

Type Of
Bambooster Battery

Bambooster AA

AA battery is a cylindrical power source commonly used in a wide range of devices, from remote controls to digital cameras. It provides 1.5 volts of electricity and is known for its versatility and widespread availability. AA batteries are compact and easy to replace, making them a popular choice for everyday electronics. They're like the unsung heroes that keep our gadgets running smoothly!

Bambooster AAA

AAA battery is a smaller version of the AA battery, but it still packs a punch! It's a cylindrical power source that provides 1.5 volts of electricity, just like its bigger sibling. AAA batteries are commonly used in smaller devices like remote controls, wireless keyboards, and portable gaming consoles. They're compact, convenient, and perfect for powering up your everyday gadgets on the go!

C battery is a smaller version of the D battery, but it still packs a punch! It's a cylindrical power source that provides 1.5 volts of electricity, just like its bigger sibling. Bambooster D

D battery is a bigger version of the C battery, but it still packs a punch! It's a cylindrical power source that provides 1.5 volts of electricity, just like its bigger sibling.
